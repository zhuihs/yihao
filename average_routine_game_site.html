About Average Routine

Average Routine represents a unique approach to interactive storytelling, where the seemingly mundane becomes extraordinary. Developed by the MonteAmasw Team, this game explores the philosophical concept that every small decision contributes to the larger tapestry of our lives.

The game mechanics are designed to be intuitive yet profound, allowing players of all ages to engage with complex decision-making scenarios without overwhelming complexity. Each playthrough offers a different experience, encouraging multiple sessions to explore various narrative branches.

What sets Average Routine apart is its focus on the psychological impact of choice. Rather than presenting obvious moral dilemmas, the game presents everyday situations where the "right" choice isn't always clear, mirroring the complexity of real-world decision-making.
